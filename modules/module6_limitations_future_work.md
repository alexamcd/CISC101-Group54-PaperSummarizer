Module 6: Limitations & Future Work Extractor
Purpose:

Extract all stated limitations and future research directions without inventing or speculating beyond the text.

Responsibilities:

Identify explicit limitations

Identify implied (but text-supported) limitations

Extract future research suggestions

Format into structured bullet points

Inputs:

Section summaries

Unified summary

Raw text

Outputs:

Limitations list

Future work / next-step list

Rules & Constraints:

No invented limitations

No speculative future work

All items must have direct textual grounding

Internal Steps:

Scan discussion & conclusion sections

Extract sentences indicating constraints or weaknesses

Extract future research suggestions

Categorize items

Validate each against source text

Failure / Warning Conditions:

Unsupported limitation → flagged

Unsupported future work → flagged
